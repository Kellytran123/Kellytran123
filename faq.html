F.A.Q.

Do I need to be home to get my delivery?

Nope! Our packages don't require a signature - this is to make sure your ice cream doesn't get shipped back to a shipping facility. If you're not home at the time of delivery, your package will be left outside your door. We strongly recommend that you unpack your pints as soon as possible to prevent unwanted thawing.

Do you accept returns?

All ice cream orders are final. Your ice cream order is always backed by our "Melt-Free Guarantee." If your ice cream doesn’t arrive frozen, we’ll reship your order and do what we can to make it right.

To contact us regarding your Gucamole Ice Cream order, please email us at: orders@gucamole.com

How can I get ice cream from your local shops?

We are now offering a contact-less way to order pints for pick-up and/or delivery directly through gucamole.com. Simply select the “Local Delivery/Pickup” option on our website, enter your preferred location, pick your ice cream, and enjoy! We have also partnered with other delivery companies such as ChowNow and Postmates.

What goes into the price of shipping?

We understand the that our shipping costs can be more than expected. Please know, however, we are actively pursuing opportunities to reduce transit costs associated with shipping our ice cream. We do not leverage third-party distribution centers to fulfill our product, and all of our ice cream is hand-made, packed, and shipped from our kitchen in Portland, Oregon - ensuring the freshest ice cream possible upon delivery. We will continue to optimize our shipping and fulfillment process and look forward to lowering these prices in the near future!

While shopping, please keep in mind that we will add shipping fees when you checkout.

How do you ship ice cream?

We ship via FedEx Overnight or 2Day Air, and for local deliveries, via FedEx Ground. We pack our pints in an insulated box with enough dry ice to ensure that the goods stay frozen for several days while in transit.
